🚧 About this project

Built by a student who kept missing out on free food and cool events because the info was scattered across five different group chats. This is a work in progress — expect rough edges, and if you spot a bug or have an idea, I'd genuinely love to hear it.

Live On Campus acknowledges the Traditional Custodians of the lands on which our campuses stand — the Wurundjeri People of the Kulin Nation (Burwood), the Wadawurrung People (Waurn Ponds), and the Eastern Maar People (Warrnambool) — and pays respect to Elders past, present, and emerging.
